Saturn World

zmiless · 2026

Saturn World is a debut album about grief, distance, and the weight of loving someone who has already left. Recorded between 2024 and 2025, the record blends shoegaze textures with raw lo-fi production — walls of guitar, buried vocals, and melodies that feel like they're dissolving.

Every song is a room in the same house. You can wander through them in order or get lost.
